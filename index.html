The Technical Architecture

How the Agentic Markup Pro Plugin Works

Agentic Markup Pro doesn't just add meta tags. It builds a dedicated, high-speed data pipeline that translates your complex WordPress database into pure AI signal.

1. Deep Database & CPT Mapping

Basic schema plugins only read standard WordPress posts. The Pro engine digs deeper, automatically mapping your custom architecture.

  • Advanced Custom Fields (ACF) Integration: Instantly exposes deeply nested ACF data—like software specs, real estate listings, or team hierarchies—to AI crawlers.
  • Custom Post Type (CPT) Support: Translates complex, non-standard post types directly into perfectly validated Schema.org entities.

2. Bypassing the Visual Web

Stop forcing bots to parse heavy page builder code. Agentic Markup Pro creates a dedicated, high-speed REST API endpoint (`/wp-json/agentic-markup/v1/feed`).

  • 100% Data Signal: Serves pure JSON-LD payloads. No CSS, no JavaScript, no visual bloat.
  • Frictionless Ingestion: Allows models like OpenAI’s SearchBot and Google AI to index your answers with zero compute penalty, drastically increasing your citation rate.

3. Automated Omni-Channel Discovery

Building the pipeline is useless if bots can't find it. The Pro version fully automates the industry's newest AI routing protocols directly from your WordPress dashboard.

  • Dynamic `llms.txt` Generation: Uses advanced rewrite rules to auto-generate the emerging standard file at your root domain, guiding polite bots to your data.
  • Custom GPT Integration (`openapi.json`): Automatically generates OpenAPI specs, allowing enterprise teams and agencies to plug your WordPress database directly into private Custom GPTs.

4. The AI Command Center

You don't need to be a developer to build an enterprise data pipeline. The Pro dashboard gives you total visual control over your AI SEO strategy directly from WordPress.

  • Automated Frontend Injection: Seamlessly drops the required AI discovery tags into your site's HTML header. Zero coding required, and it never breaks your visual theme.
  • Visual Health Dashboard: Monitor your exact AI readiness score with real-time donut charts that calculate your total Knowledge Graph coverage and validation status.
